Rebonding

Do you have curly hair and wish to change them to poker straight? If yes then hair rebonding the best available option for you. It is a process in which hair bonds are broken making use of a chemical formula and then experts apply neutralizers for restructuring them. We, at Makeover Salons, strive hard to enhance your hair rebonding experience. Our methods are just designed for treating Indian hair texture. Our hair experts take special care in ensuring that you are more than satisfied with our hair rebonding service

Perming

Some hairstyles always remain in fashion. Perming is one such popular hairstyle. Curly hair has always been prized, sexy and adorable. Whether you want a trendy hairstyle or want to change the look just to break the monotony, a perm is the way to go. Our stylists make sure that you get a vivid and rich look.
