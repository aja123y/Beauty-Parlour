Makeup & Beauty Services

Reception Makeup

Reception makeup can make a world of difference to your overall look and personality. For a bride, it is the day when she is introduced to friends and relatives. Our professional make-up artists make sure you look at your best on this special day.

Engagement Makeup

Is your engagement a few weeks away? Haven’t you yet decided the make-up you would opt for? Well, we can come to your rescue. Engagement is the first step towards sharing a bond which lasts forever; hence you should look charming, elegant and attractive. We offer different types of engagement makeup so that you can choose the best accordingly.

Party Makeup

It’s time to rock and unwind. It’s party time. Is your best friends or sister all set to tie the nuptial knot? Perhaps, you wish to look really sexy and hot at a Cocktail party being thrown by your close friend? We will give you just the perfect look for any occasion. Look glamorous and chic on any party you want to attend.

Bridal Makeup

Who does not want to look the best perhaps on the most important day of your life – your wedding? Bridal makeup encompasses the true spirit of marriage. From bold eyes to subtle undertones, we understand how the bride wants to be on the most important event of her life. Tailored according to the needs of the bride-to-be, our bridal makeup lends a class apart look.

Facials

When it comes to facials, when done regularly, it can make a world of difference to your quality of skin and texture. It prevents appearance of early signs of ageing. All our facial products leave your skin radiant, flawless, supple, healthy and toned.

Waxing

Long gone are the times of painful waxing as today you have plenty of wax options to get rid of unwanted hair from your body. From regular wax to chocolate and oil wax, there are options available for you. Our experts make sure you feel the least pain and discomfort while waxing.

Pedicure and Manicure

Pamper yourself by indulging in manicure and pedicure. Professionals at our salon make sure that the results of these two services leave your hands and feet smooth and clean. There are several branded products from which you can pick the one as per your preference and budget.

Threading

Unwanted facial hair growth can be the reason for embarrassment. Threading is one of the most common methods of getting rid of excess eyebrows hair. Women from all walks of life and across all age groups opt for this service.

Body Massage

In the present times of hectic and packed work schedule, stress often takes toll on life. The best way to relax and rejuvenate mind and body is to spare out a few minutes and enjoy the benefits of body massage.

Head Massage

Hair care is important for all and sundry. Pollution, exposure to sun and hair care products often leaves your hair and scalp dry. A head massage is the best answer to feel relaxed while improving the quality of hair.
